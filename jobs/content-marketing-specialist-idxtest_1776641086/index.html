Content Marketing Specialist

Remote Works Ltd · London, England · Full-time

We're looking for a mid-level Content Marketing Specialist to join our growing editorial team.

This role is a great fit if you're passionate about the entertainment industry and want to work across a portfolio of active publications. For context on the kind of coverage we produce, see our recent feature on Apple TV's upcoming political thriller The Savant, a good example of our editorial tone and depth.

What you'll do

  • Research and write long-form articles covering streaming platforms, film releases, and entertainment news
  • Collaborate with the SEO team to optimise existing content
  • Manage an editorial calendar across 3-4 niche sites
  • Interview industry contacts and turn conversations into publishable features

What we're looking for

  • 2+ years experience in content marketing, journalism or editorial
  • Portfolio of published long-form writing
  • Working knowledge of SEO basics
  • Familiarity with WordPress or similar CMS

Nice to have

  • Interest in film and television industry
  • Experience with Ahrefs or Semrush

Compensation

GBP 42,000 – 58,000 per year, depending on experience. Full-time, permanent position.

How to apply

Send a CV and two writing samples to the hiring team via our careers inbox. We review applications on a rolling basis.
