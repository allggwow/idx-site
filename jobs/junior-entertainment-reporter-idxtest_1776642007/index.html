Junior Entertainment Reporter

Pixelbeam Media · Manchester, England · Full-time

Pixelbeam Media is hiring a Junior Entertainment Reporter to cover new releases across streaming services.

This role is a great fit if you're passionate about the entertainment industry and want to work across a portfolio of active publications. For context on the kind of coverage we produce, see our recent feature on Apple TV's upcoming political thriller The Savant, a good example of our editorial tone and depth.

What you'll do

  • File 4-6 news stories per week on film and series announcements
  • Monitor press releases and industry trades for breaking news
  • Attend virtual press junkets and write up interviews
  • Work with the video team on short-form social content

What we're looking for

  • Degree in journalism, communications or equivalent experience
  • At least one published writing sample
  • Strong grasp of entertainment industry current events
  • Comfortable working to tight deadlines

Nice to have

  • Shorthand qualification
  • Second language

Compensation

GBP 28,500 – 34,200 per year, depending on experience. Full-time, permanent position.

How to apply

Send a CV and two writing samples to the hiring team via our careers inbox. We review applications on a rolling basis.
